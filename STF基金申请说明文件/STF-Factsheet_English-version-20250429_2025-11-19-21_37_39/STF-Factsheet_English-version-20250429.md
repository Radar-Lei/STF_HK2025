Smart Traffic Fund

The Transport Department has set up a \$1 billion Smart Traffic Fund to provide funding support for research and application projects on innovation and technology to enhance the convenience, efficiency and safety of the commuting public.

## Overview of the Fund

<table><tr><td>Scope</td><td>Support enterprises or organisations to conduct research and application on vehicle-related innovation and technology with at least one of the following objectives: <br> - enhancing convenience of motorists <br> - enhancing efficiency of the road network or road space <br> - improving driving safety</td></tr><tr><td>Eligibility</td><td>Accept applications from companies, research institutions, professional bodies trade associations and non-government organisations Applicants are categorized into corporate sector or public sector</td></tr><tr><td>Maximum Fund's Contribution</td><td>50% of the Estimated Project Cost for corporate sector <br> 90% of the Estimated Project Cost for public sector <br> Joint Application <br> 70% of the Estimated Project Cost for application submitted jointly by Applicants from corporate sector and Public Sector Research Institutions*</td></tr><tr><td>Funding Ceiling per Project</td><td>Research & Application Project: HK\$20,000,000 <br> Pure Research Project: HK\$8,000,000 <br> (including project audit fee)</td></tr><tr><td>Maximum Number of Projects per Applicant</td><td>Applicant can apply for funding for multiple projects However, each project should be distinct</td></tr><tr><td>Project Duration</td><td>Generally not exceed 24 months</td></tr><tr><td>Submission of Reports</td><td>The applicant is required to submit progress report(s) as well as interim report(s) until project completion <br> On project completion, the applicant is required to submit a final report</td></tr></table>
